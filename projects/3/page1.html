Who is Reggie?

Reggie Fils-Aimé is an American businessman best known for being the president and chief operating officer of Nintendo of America, the North American branch of the Japanese video game company Nintendo, from 2006 to 2019. Fils-Aimé is revered by business leaders and gaming fans alike for helping bring Nintendo’s greatest successes – including the Nintendo DS, the Wii, and the Nintendo Switch – to the global marketplace. A proven expert on transforming companies, re-vitalizing brands, and reshaping industries, he is credited with revamping Nintendo’s public relations, bringing their “cool” factor back with unique products that no other game company had thought of.

Reginald Fils-Aimé was born on March 25, 1961, in The Bronx, New York City. His parents had immigrated to the United States from Haiti in the 1950s due to conflicting political views of his grandparents. Reggie has documented his journey in his self-biography Disrupting the Game: From the Bronx to the Top of Nintendo. His book can be found on Amazon, and is an inspiration to many young minds with the addition of personal thoughts, reflections, and advice for anyone striving to be successful in the industry and life.

Recreational Projects

I really enjoy learning how things work and customizing my experience with games and programs! Sometimes I'll mess around in blender, creating and editing 3D fictional characters.

A project I did back in high school was attempting to add my best friend to Super Smash Bros. Brawl for the Wii using a custom made 3D model made from scratch and community tools for hacking and modding the mentioned game. I tracked my journey with this Wix Site.

Unfortunately, despite my best efforts, I was not able to make him playable within the game but I did end up with a 3D model of him. Years later, I undusted his model and attempted to add him to the newest iteration of the game using community tools and guides. Here are pictures of him in action!

More fun stuff to be added soon!
